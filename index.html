About Me

Just a kid
who feels deeply.

I'm Elijah Cabanero — a BSIT student at National University Manila who spends the quiet hours doing what he loves most: working with sound.

I'm not a professional, and I'm not trying to be famous. I'm just someone who found that music is the one language that always makes sense—especially Brent Faiyaz, with the way he layers emotion into frequency. But growing up, it was the raw energy of NWA and 90s boom bap that really hooked me. Hearing how those classic tracks were built is exactly what drove me to download FL Studio at a young age and start figuring out how to make beats myself.

So between classes and code, I beat match, cut up, remix, and make my own stuff. This is that.
